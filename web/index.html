How it works

From headlines to broadcast in four steps

1

Firecrawl gathers news

Searches today's headlines via Firecrawl's news endpoint, filtered by region and date.

2

Firecrawl verifies facts

Each claim is cross-searched for fact-checks. Unverified stories get flagged on air.

3

AI writes the script

Claude picks the top stories and rewrites them in each era's authentic broadcast voice.

4

ElevenLabs delivers it

Text-to-Dialogue for dual-anchor TV. Language-native TTS for radio. Plus a Conversational AI Agent you can call live.

ElevenLabs Agent × Firecrawl

4 Firecrawl tools your anchor calls live

search_news

Pulls today's headlines by topic and region. Ask "What happened with…?" and this fires.

fc.search(query, sources: news)
fact_check

Cross-checks a claim against fact-checking sources. Ask "Is it true that…?" to trigger it.

fc.search(claim + "fact check")
read_article

Scrapes the full text of any article URL so the anchor can quote the original source back to you.

fc.scrape_url(url, markdown)
search_topic

Runs a general web search on any topic. Go off-script and ask about anything beyond the news.

fc.search(query)
